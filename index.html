Welcome to my personal webpage! I am a PhD student at the Department of Atmospheric Science, Colorado State University, working with Dr. Peter Jan van Leeuwen. My recent research topic is extending the application of the axisymmetric tropical cyclone theory to better explain the steady-state tropical cyclone intensity and structure in observations and model simulations.
